We are one of the largest child-focused humanitarian organisations in the country. We use proven, effective development, public engagement, and relief techniques to enable vulnerable children and communities living in poverty and injustice to become self-sufficient and deliver permanent change, drawing on over six decades of grassroots experience. As a display of Christ's unconditional love for all people, we serve all children regardless of faith, colour, nationality, or gender. With your help, we can create a country that is safe for children. Please join us. For the sake of the children, we have come together. For the sake of change. For the rest of your life.

Grass-Root Based

The relief and development work by Us is community-based. Our staff reside with the communities at the grassroots - live with them, learn from them, listen to them.

Focus On Children

All development work that we perform focus on children - the bright young minds of tomorrow.

Partnering For Change

We partner with communities, children, the Government, civil society, corporations, academia and faith-based organisations to build a nation ideal for children.
